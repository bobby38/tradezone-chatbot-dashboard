## Important Notes

1. **Condition Grading**:
   - Mint: Like new, no scratches, original box
   - Good: Minor wear, fully functional
   - Fair: Visible wear, fully functional
   - Faulty: Hardware issues, discounted further

2. **Pricing Subject to Inspection**: All trade-in values are estimates. Final value determined after physical inspection at store.

3. **Storage Variants**: Always confirm storage capacity (64GB, 512GB, 1TB, 2TB) as it significantly affects pricing.

4. **Digital vs Disc**: For PS5, Xbox - always ask which edition as pricing differs by S$50-100.
